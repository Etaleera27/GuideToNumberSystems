Conversion for Decimal

Decimal to Binary

217₁₀ to Binary (Using Method 1: Division/Multiplication)

Step 1: Divide the number by 2 repeatedly

217 ÷ 2 = 108 remainder 1
108 ÷ 2 = 54 remainder 0
54 ÷ 2 = 27 remainder 0
27 ÷ 2 = 13 remainder 1
13 ÷ 2 = 6 remainder 1
6 ÷ 2 = 3 remainder 0
3 ÷ 2 = 1 remainder 1
1 ÷ 2 = 0 remainder 1

Step 2: Write the remainders from bottom to top

11011001₂
Answer: 217₁₀ = 11011001₂

Decimal to Octal

45₁₀ to Octal (Using Method 1: Division/Multiplication)

Step 1: Divide the number by 8 repeatedly

45 ÷ 8 = 5 remainder 5
5 ÷ 8 = 0 remainder 5

Step 2: Write the remainders from bottom to top

55₈
Answer: 45₁₀ = 55₈

Decimal to Hexadecimal

45₁₀ to Hexadecimal (Using Method 1: Division/Multiplication)

Step 1: Divide the number by 16 repeatedly

45 ÷ 16 = 2 remainder 13
2 ÷ 16 = 0 remainder 2

Step 2: Convert remainder to hexadecimal

  • 13 → D

Step 3: Write the remainders from bottom to top

2D₁₆
Answer: 45₁₀ = 2D₁₆
